Boursin Baked Eggs

Ingredients
4 Servings

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (5.3 ounce) package Boursin Garlic and Fine Herbs cheese
  • 8 large eggs
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (180 degrees C).
    Spray a 9x9 baking dish with cooking spray.

  • STEP 2

    Place block of Boursin in the center of the baking dish.

  • STEP 3

    Crack 8 eggs carefully into the baking dish around the block of Boursin.

  • STEP 4

    Bake in the preheated oven until the egg white around the block of Boursin is cooked through,
    15 minutes. Yolks may be slightly runny.

  • STEP 5

    Using a fork, mash the eggs and Boursin together until until well combined.
    to taste with salt and pepper.
