Air Fryer Miso-Glazed Salmon

Ingredients
2 Servings

  • 2 (6 ounce) salmon filets
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili flakes
  • cooking spray

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Place salmon fillets in a flat dish.

  • STEP 2

    Whisk brown sugar, miso paste, soy sauce, and chili flakes in a bowl.
    Pour mixture over salmon fillets. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

  • STEP 3

    Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray basket with cooking spray.

  • STEP 4

    Using tongs, place salmon filets skin side down in the basket of the air fryer,
    sure to reserve the excess marinade. Cook 4 minutes. Baste filets with reserved marinade
    and cook 4 minutes more.
