High Protein Cottage Cheese Mac and Cheese

Ingredients
1 Servings

  • 1/2 cup high protein elbow pasta, such as Banza elbows chickpea pasta
  • 1/2 cup high protein cottage cheese, such as Good Culture
  • 1 ounce Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/2 ounce Parmigiano Reggiano cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese powder (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons high protein 2% milk, such as Fa!rlife
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons everything seasoning blend

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
    Add pasta and cook until tender with a bite, about 7 minutes.

  • STEP 2

    Meanwhile in a food processor or high-powered blender add cottage cheese, Cheddar cheese,
    Reggiano, Cheddar cheese powder, Worcestershire, Dijon,
    and milk. Season with salt and pepper. Blend until smooth.

  • STEP 3

    Drain pasta, and return pasta to pan. Pour in cheese sauce.
    Add everything bagel seasoning; stir to combine. Serve immediately.
