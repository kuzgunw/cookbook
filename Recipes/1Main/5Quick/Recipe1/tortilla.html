Air Fryer Tortilla Quiche

Ingredients
2 Servings

  • air fryer liner
  • 1 large (burrito-size) tortilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup spinach, torn into pieces, or to taste
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Line your air fryer with an air fryer liner. Place tortilla into the air fryer, so the sides are covered.

  • STEP 2

    Whisk eggs and cottage cheese together in a bowl.
    Pour into the tortilla in the air fryer and season with salt and pepper.
    Sprinkle with bell pepper and spinach. Top with cheese.

  • STEP 3

    Air fry at 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) until eggs are set, about 20 minutes.
    Remove, allow to cool for a few minutes, quarter, and serve.
