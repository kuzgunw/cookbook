Sugar Cookie Pumpkin Cupcake Muffins

Ingredients
12 Servings

  • 1 (16.5 ounce) roll refrigerated sugar cookie dough
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup self-rising flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 cup cream cheese frosting

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
    Line a standard 12-well muffin tin with paper liners.

  • STEP 2

    Add cookie dough, pumpkin puree, flour, eggs, and cinnamon to a large mixing bowl.
    Blend until smooth with an electric mixer. Divide batter evenly among 12 lined muffin cups.

  • STEP 3

    Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean,
    about 20 minutes. Let cool completely., about 20 minutes.

  • STEP 4

    Top each cupcake with cream cheese frosting.
